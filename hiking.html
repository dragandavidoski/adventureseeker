DO'S
  • Choose a hunting spot with good access and safety.
  • Bring the right equipment and gear for your target game.
  • Observe local hunting regulations and obtain necessary permits.
  • Practice ethical hunting practices and respect wildlife.
  • Keep your hunting area clean and dispose of waste properly.
DO NOT'S
  • Disregard firearm safety rules and guidelines.
  • Hunt on private property without permission.
  • Forget to notify others of your hunting plans.
  • Leave game meat exposed to the elements.
  • Pollute the hunting spot with trash or debris.
