DO'S
  • Choose a fishing spot with good access and safety.
  • Bring the right bait and tackle for the fish you're targeting.
  • Observe local fishing regulations and obtain necessary permits.
  • Practice catch and release when necessary.
  • Keep your fishing area clean and dispose of waste properly.
DO NOT'S
  • Leave fishing lines or hooks unattended.
  • Forget to check the weather and tides.
  • Ignore local fishing limits and guidelines.
  • Leave caught fish exposed to the sun.
  • Pollute the fishing spot with trash or debris.
