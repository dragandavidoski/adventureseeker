DO'S
  • Choose a suitable campsite with access to water and facilities.
  • Bring appropriate clothing and gear for the weather conditions.
  • Set up your campsite away from wildlife habitats and water sources.
  • Dispose of waste properly and leave no trace of your visit.
  • Follow fire safety guidelines and regulations.
DO NOT'S
  • Leave food unattended or accessible to wildlife.
  • Forget to check the weather forecast before your trip.
  • Ignore campground rules and regulations.
  • Leave your campfire unattended.
  • Leave trash or waste behind.
